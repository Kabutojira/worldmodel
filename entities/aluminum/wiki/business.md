# Aluminum Business

## Core model

- Pricing reflects power costs, lightweighting demand, and supply discipline in smelting.
- Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: EV, grid, and solar input.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes Aluminum demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Solar energy: Solar energy shapes Aluminum demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Electricity grid: Electricity grid is a physical bottleneck or enabling network that influences Aluminum scaling, utilization, and returns.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: aluminum, lightweighting, power cables, auto aluminum.
- Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
