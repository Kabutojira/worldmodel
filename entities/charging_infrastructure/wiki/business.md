# EV charging infrastructure Business

## Core model

- EV charging infrastructure should be tracked through demand drivers, supply constraints, pricing, and leading indicators.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: EV adoption bottleneck and Tesla network asset.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Tesla: Tesla is a strategically relevant peer, supplier, customer, or dependency that changes EV charging infrastructure economics and optionality.
- Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes EV charging infrastructure demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Electricity grid: Electricity grid is a physical bottleneck or enabling network that influences EV charging infrastructure scaling, utilization, and returns.
- Energy: Energy shapes EV charging infrastructure demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: EV charging, Supercharger, NACS, fast charging.
- Track leading indicators, capex announcements, utilization, and regulatory milestones.
