# Natural gas Business

## Core model

- Pricing reflects storage, weather, LNG exports, power burn, and data-center-driven electricity demand.
- Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: Flexible power source for data-center load growth.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Energy: Energy shapes Natural gas demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- AI data centers: AI data centers is a physical bottleneck or enabling network that influences Natural gas scaling, utilization, and returns.
- Electricity grid: Electricity grid is a physical bottleneck or enabling network that influences Natural gas scaling, utilization, and returns.
- Oil: Oil affects Natural gas cost, supply security, or pricing through critical material exposure and procurement risk.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: natural gas, LNG, gas turbines, power generation.
- Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
