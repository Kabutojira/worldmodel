# TSMC

## Overview

TSMC remains the tightest public-market manufacturing bottleneck inside the AI stack. The latest accessible evidence did not come from TSMC IR directly; it came from Taiwan macro data showing that AI-chip demand is now large enough to distort the country's growth profile.[^taiwan]

## Current thesis summary

- **Demand signal strengthened:** Taiwan's late-2025 growth and export surge is consistent with extraordinary AI-led semiconductor pull, which supports the view that TSMC capacity remains structurally scarce.[^taiwan]
- **What changed:** the investable read-through is less about whether AI demand is real and more about how concentrated it remains in Taiwan.
- **Risk signal also strengthened:** geopolitical concentration risk is not fading as fast as the market may want to believe.
