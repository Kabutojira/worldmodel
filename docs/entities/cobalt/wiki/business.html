Cobalt Business

Core model

  • Pricing is driven by battery cathode demand, DRC supply risk, and chemistry shifts away from cobalt intensity.
  • Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

Revenue / value drivers

  • Primary thesis link: Battery commodity with ethical/supply risk.
  • Connected-entity mechanisms:
  • Battery energy storage: Battery energy storage shapes Cobalt demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
  • Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes Cobalt demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
  • Nickel: Nickel affects Cobalt cost, supply security, or pricing through critical material exposure and procurement risk.

What to update in daily runs

  • Metrics to monitor: cobalt, DRC cobalt, battery cathode, NMC.
  • Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
