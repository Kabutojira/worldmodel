# Graphite Business

## Core model

- Pricing and supply risk hinge on anode demand growth, synthetic-vs-natural mix, and China processing concentration.
- Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: Battery anode bottleneck and China exposure.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Battery energy storage: Battery energy storage shapes Graphite demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes Graphite demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: graphite, battery anode, synthetic graphite, natural graphite.
- Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
