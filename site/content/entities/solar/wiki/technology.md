# Solar energy Technology

## Technical / system bottlenecks

- Demand drivers: falling module costs, PPA demand, rooftop/utility economics.
- Constraints: interconnection, interest rates, silver/copper inputs, land/permitting.

## Dependency chain

- Energy: Energy shapes Solar energy demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Battery energy storage: Battery energy storage shapes Solar energy demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Electricity grid: Electricity grid is a physical bottleneck or enabling network that influences Solar energy scaling, utilization, and returns.
- Silver: Silver affects Solar energy cost, supply security, or pricing through critical material exposure and procurement risk.
- Copper: Copper affects Solar energy cost, supply security, or pricing through critical material exposure and procurement risk.
