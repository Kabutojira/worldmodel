# Nickel Business

## Core model

- Pricing follows stainless and battery demand, Indonesia supply growth, and chemistry substitution toward LFP.
- Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: Battery and stainless commodity; chemistry shift indicator.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Battery energy storage: Battery energy storage shapes Nickel demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes Nickel demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Tesla: Tesla is a strategically relevant peer, supplier, customer, or dependency that changes Nickel economics and optionality.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: nickel, battery nickel, class 1 nickel, NMC batteries.
- Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
