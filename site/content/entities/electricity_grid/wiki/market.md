# Electricity grid Market

## Market map

- Demand drivers: electrification, data centers, EV charging, renewables interconnection.
- Constraints: transformers, converters/inverters/MMCs, transmission build times, permitting, labor.

## Latest evidence

- ChinaTalk notes China has built more high-voltage transmission in the last fifteen years than the US has in its history, with 45 UHV projects operating by late 2025 and more than 40,000 km of UHVDC lines after the 14th Five-Year Plan period.<a href="https://www.chinatalk.media/p/transmission-dominance-with-chinese">source</a>
- The same source family argues the grid bottleneck is broader than transformers: converters, inverters, HVDC components, and modular multilevel converters remain meaningful supply constraints even if transformer supply improves.<a href="https://www.chinatalk.media/p/yes-transformers-are-a-problem">source</a>
- Implication: US AI-power and electrification timelines depend not just on transformer availability but on a deeper power-electronics stack where China currently has more manufacturing and deployment momentum.
