# Rare earths Business

## Core model

- Pricing and strategic risk depend on permanent-magnet demand, China processing dominance, and defense/clean-tech demand.
- Bootstrap focus: price benchmark, supply additions, inventory / processing constraints, and downstream demand.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: Motors, robotics, wind, and China supply-chain risk.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Electric vehicle market: Electric vehicle market shapes Rare earths demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Humanoid robots: Humanoid robots shapes Rare earths demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.
- Wind power: Wind power shapes Rare earths demand, pricing, market share, or capital allocation through adoption, competition, and cycle conditions.

## What to update in daily runs

- Metrics to monitor: rare earths, neodymium, magnets, permanent magnets.
- Track benchmark prices, project ramps, processing concentration, and substitution trends.
