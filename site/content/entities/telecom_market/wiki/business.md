# Telecom market Business

## Core model

- Demand drivers: data usage, broadband substitution, enterprise connectivity.
- Constraints: spectrum, capex, pricing competition, regulation.

## Revenue / value drivers

- Primary thesis link: Connectivity TAM and substitution risk.
- Connected-entity mechanisms:
- Starlink: Starlink is a strategically relevant peer, supplier, customer, or dependency that changes Telecom market economics and optionality.
- Amazon Kuiper: Amazon Kuiper is a strategically relevant peer, supplier, customer, or dependency that changes Telecom market economics and optionality.
